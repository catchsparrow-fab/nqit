Stories are always the same; it's only the names that change.

And nQit knows all the stories.

Artificial Intelligence in your office.

nQit is a state-of-the-art AI that analyzes data from millions of candidates and companies and applies machine learning, deep data mining, and pattern recognition to identify best candidates for your organization.

Big data

Harness the power of big data. nQit uses millions of data points from multiple data sources, such as demographic data, public domain archives, web search, and social networks.

Customization

nQit is tailored to your organization. It helps you find the perfect candidate for your company, not just "a perfect candidate". (There is no such thing.)

Adaptation

nQit is incredibly versatile. It constantly acquires new data and uses it to learn and improve the accuracy of its predictions. And it's doing it 24/7, 365 days a year.

Privacy

Your usage of nQit starts with a privacy agreement that guarantees that under no circumstances any data about your organization or employees will ever become available to a third party.

How it works

You enter details about your organization (just once, unless anything changes in future). When you're ready to ask nQit about a candidate, you enter their information into the system, and nQit comes back with predictions on various topics, made based on the patterns nQit has learned about thousands and thousands of similar candidates and companies. Most importantly, it tells you if it thinks the candidate would be a good fit for the position you have in mind.

FAQ

What makes nQit different?

Instead of relying solely on the information provided in the resume and during the interview (which may or may not be accurate), nQit compares the candidate against common patterns it has learned about the labour market. By using subtle cues and regularities that are not always evident to human eye, nQit establishes connections that help make predictions about the candidate's future behaviour.

How much does it cost?

In North America, after all is said and done, an average candidate costs about $2,200 to hire, including fees paid to recruiting agencies. nQit helps you make the right decision in choosing the perfect candidate, a crucial decision that affects your organization in the long-term. And with our Ultimate package, it only costs you about $3.3 per candidate.

How to install nQit?

nQit is offered as a software-as-a-service. It means no installation is required in your organization; we host and configure it for you. nQit is available as a web application and mobile apps for Android and iOS devices. Your data is protected by a strict privacy agreement that guarantees that, although your data is used anonymously for machine learning purposes, nQit will never share it directly with a third party.
